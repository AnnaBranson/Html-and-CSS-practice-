Jane Eyre

The Woman Who Waited

A lesson in patience, virtue and kindness

Jane is a orphan. She begins her life very much unloved by anyone around her. Though as she grows and learns more about herself and her values, she finds strength in her character and those around her see her for who she truely is. By the end of her story, she has a family who love her, money to keep her and the love of her life by her side. It is truely a happy ending.
